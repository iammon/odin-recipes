Pork Chop

Description

This is a simple pork chop recipe that we all can make in in our air fryer with only a few ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Pork chop slab
  • Adobo seasoning
  • Cooking oil
  • Tongs
  • Air fryer

Steps

  1. Brush pork chop with cooking oil
  2. Season oiled pork chop with Adobo seasoning
  3. Pre-heat air fryer to 375 degree Fahrenheit
  4. Place seasoned pork chop in air fryer for 10 minutes
  5. After 10 minutes, flip the pork chop and cook for another 10 minutes
  6. Take out of air fryer to enjoy whole or cut in small chunks
