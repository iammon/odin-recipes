Burger

Description

This is a simple burger recipe that we all can make in our air fryer with only five ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Pre-made burger patty
  • Burger buns
  • Jar of pickle slices
  • Tube of mayonnaise
  • Tube of ketchup
  • Tongs

Steps

  1. Pre-heat air fryer to 375 degree Fahrenheit
  2. Place one burger patty in air fryer for 4 minutes
  3. Use tongs to flip the patty and air fry for 4 more minutes
  4. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on each buns respectively
  5. Take patty out air fryer and place on the bun
  6. Put 3 slices of pickle on top of patty
  7. Put the other bun on top of the patty to complete the recipe
