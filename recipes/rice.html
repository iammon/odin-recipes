Steamed Rice

Description

This is a simple steamed rice recipe that we all can make in a rice cooker with one ingredient!

Ingredients

  • Jasmine rice
  • Rice cooker

Steps

  1. Put 5 cups of Jasmine rice in the pot that comes with the rice cooker
  2. Rinse the starch out of the rice with water until rice water is almost clear
  3. Put water in the pot until water level reaches the number 5 as indicated on the inside of the rice pot (5 because we used 5 cups but that changes based on how many cups you are cooking)
  4. Dry the rice pot with a towel and place inside the rice cooker
  5. Close the lid on the rice cooker and flip the switch to cook
  6. Rice cooker will automatically cook the rice until it's ready (~20 minutes)
